  • Competition-related information;
  • Bout results;
  • Wins and losses;
  • Final rankings and placements;
  • Sports statistics;
  • Information entered by clubs, organizers, or responsible users.

2.2 Account information

When applicable, we may collect:

  • Account identifiers;
  • Information required for authentication;

2.3 Technical data

We may collect technical information required for the operation, maintenance, and security of the Application, including:

  • Device type;
  • Operating system;
  • Application version;
  • Error diagnostic information.

3. How We Use Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Create and manage fencing competitions;
  • Organize pools and elimination rounds;
  • Record bout results;
  • Generate classifications;
  • Create athlete histories;
  • Generate rankings;
  • Produce documents and PDF files;
  • Improve application performance, stability, and security;
  • Manage Premium features.

Registered sports data is used exclusively for functionalities related to competition organization and analysis.

Fencingly does not sell, rent, or commercialize users' personal data.

  • When requested by the user, for example through sharing PDFs or documents generated by the Application;

The user responsible for registering athletes declares that they have the appropriate authorization to enter this information into the Application.

6. Data Storage and Security

Fencingly adopts technical and organizational measures to protect stored information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

7. Premium Subscriptions

Fencing Competitions may offer Premium features through subscription plans.

Users are responsible for sharing these documents and for the information contained within them.
